# Code of Conduct

Krititva AI adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) verbatim.

## Enforcement

Report violations to `conduct@krititva.dev` (or, until that alias is stood up: **aditya.tripathi@echelonedge.com** with subject `CoC: <short description>`).

All reports are handled confidentially. Consequences follow the Contributor Covenant enforcement guidelines: correction, warning, temporary ban, permanent ban — proportional to the severity of the violation and pattern of behavior.
